US Troops in Iraq

Iraq is one of the countries in the Middle East that the US has deployed its most troops in the 21st century.

By Your Name

Beginning in 2003, the United States of America deployed massive numbers of troops to Iraq. This was in order to forcibly dismantle Saddam Husseins regime. The sustained presence was driven by the desire to transform the middle east. US forces peaked in 2007 with 170,000 troops.
